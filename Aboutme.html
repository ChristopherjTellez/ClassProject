About me!



My name is Christopher James Tellez, I am originally from San Diego California and I have been in the Air Force for almost 11 years. I was stationed at Nellis AFB for a long 9 years working on F-15C/D and E models before I made it out to Yokota AFB in Japan where I am currently stationed working on C-130J's. I was really excited to be able to go to Japan and have enjoyed my stay so far. As far is my future is concerned, I'm planning to stay until I hit my 20 year mark and retiring.
